# Research Skills

Operational competencies for finding, evaluating, and synthesizing information into
trustworthy, cited conclusions. Every skill here follows the house style in
[../skill-builder/SKILL.md](../skill-builder/SKILL.md).

## Design philosophy

Research skills share one discipline: **conclusions must be grounded in evaluated
evidence and traceable to sources.** Every skill emphasizes source quality,
explicit uncertainty, and separating what the evidence supports from what the
researcher wishes were true. They pair with [../review/reviewing-research](../review/reviewing-research/SKILL.md)
for appraisal and with the deep-research harness for large multi-source efforts.

## The skills

**Gathering & evaluating**
| Skill | Produces |
|---|---|
| [collecting-evidence](collecting-evidence/SKILL.md) | A structured, traceable evidence base |
| [assessing-source-credibility](assessing-source-credibility/SKILL.md) | A credibility judgment per source |
| [verifying-facts](verifying-facts/SKILL.md) | Confirmed / refuted / unverifiable claims |
| [reading-scientific-papers](reading-scientific-papers/SKILL.md) | Fast, accurate extraction from a paper |
| [citing-sources](citing-sources/SKILL.md) | Correct, consistent citations |

**Structured investigation**
| Skill | Produces |
|---|---|
| [conducting-literature-reviews](conducting-literature-reviews/SKILL.md) | A synthesized review of a body of work |
| [conducting-comparative-research](conducting-comparative-research/SKILL.md) | A like-for-like comparison |
| [analyzing-competitors](analyzing-competitors/SKILL.md) | A competitive landscape + implications |
| [conducting-market-research](conducting-market-research/SKILL.md) | Market size, segments, and demand insight |
| [evaluating-technology](evaluating-technology/SKILL.md) | A technology fit assessment |

**Synthesis**
| Skill | Produces |
|---|---|
| [synthesizing-research](synthesizing-research/SKILL.md) | An integrated, cited answer across sources |

## How they compose

- `collecting-evidence` → `assessing-source-credibility` + `verifying-facts` →
  `synthesizing-research` → cite with `citing-sources`.
- `conducting-literature-reviews` and `reading-scientific-papers` feed
  `synthesizing-research`.
- `analyzing-competitors`, `conducting-market-research`, and `evaluating-technology`
  feed business decisions ([../business/writing-business-cases](../business/writing-business-cases/SKILL.md),
  [../business/building-decision-matrices](../business/building-decision-matrices/SKILL.md)).
- Appraise any study with [../review/reviewing-research](../review/reviewing-research/SKILL.md).
- For a large, multi-source, fact-checked report, use the built-in **deep-research**
  harness; these skills define the underlying disciplines it applies.
