A simple guide for everyone

Your everyday helpers

Small assistants that do the writing and paperwork you repeat every week. Just ask in your own words.

How to use any of them

1

Open Claudeand start a message.

2

Add what you havepaste your notes, or attach the document or spreadsheet.

3

Ask in plain Englishsay what you want, the way you'd ask a colleague.

4

Review & tweakif it's not quite right, just say what to change.

No commands to memorize. Claude picks the right helper for you automatically.

Writing & communication

📝

Meeting summaries

Turns messy meeting notes or a transcript into a clear summary: what was decided, who is doing what by when, and what's still open.

Just say

Summarize these meeting notes. What did we decide, and who owns what?
✉️

Email helper

Writes, replies to, or improves work emails in a professional tone — and offers a couple of versions so you can pick the one that fits.

Just say

Write an email to the client explaining the delay. Reply to this and politely decline. Make this email shorter and friendlier.
📣

Change announcements

Writes a clear announcement or release note: what's changing, why, who is affected, and what they need to do.

Just say

Write a note announcing the new expense policy. Turn this list of updates into release notes.

Reports & documents

📊

Status updates

Turns your scattered updates into a tidy status report with a simple red / amber / green health flag — for your team, a manager, or a client.

Just say

Turn these updates into this week's status report. Write a client-friendly progress update from my notes.
🎨

Branded documents

Produces a polished Word or PDF in your company's standard look, with the correct client logo already in place.

Just say

Create this week's branded project update for Globex.
Your team sets up the templates and logos once, so everyone's documents match.
📈

Data reports

Turns a spreadsheet into a shareable report with the key numbers and charts — a single file you can email, no attachments to manage.

Just say

Make a report from this sales spreadsheet. Turn this data into charts I can share.
A one-time setup by your team enables this.
📄

Document summaries

Reads a long document, contract, or PDF and gives you the key points, the risks to watch, and answers to your questions — in plain language.

Just say

Summarize this contract in one page. What are the risks in this document? Does this PDF mention cancellation fees?

Your brand

📘

Brand guide

Creates or updates your brand style guide — colours, fonts, logo rules, and tone of voice — in one clear document everyone can follow.

Just say

Write a brand guide from our logo and these three colours. Add a tone-of-voice section to our style guide.

Tip: if you're not sure which helper you need, just describe your task — Claude will choose.
