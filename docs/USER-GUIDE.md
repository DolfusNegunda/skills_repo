# Your Everyday Helpers — Simple Guide

Small assistants that do the writing and paperwork you repeat every week.
**No commands to memorize** — just ask in your own words.

> Prefer a nicer page? Open **[user-guide.html](user-guide.html)** in any web browser
> (double-click the file). This is the same guide in plain text.

## How to use any of them

1. **Open Claude** and start a message.
2. **Add what you have** — paste your notes, or attach the document or spreadsheet.
3. **Ask in plain English** — say what you want, the way you'd ask a colleague.
4. **Review and tweak** — if it's not quite right, just say what to change.

Claude picks the right helper for you automatically.

## Writing & communication

### 📝 Meeting summaries
Turns messy meeting notes or a transcript into a clear summary: what was decided,
who is doing what by when, and what's still open.
- *"Summarize these meeting notes."*
- *"What did we decide, and who owns what?"*

### ✉️ Email helper
Writes, replies to, or improves work emails in a professional tone — and offers a
couple of versions so you can pick the one that fits.
- *"Write an email to the client explaining the delay."*
- *"Reply to this and politely decline."*
- *"Make this email shorter and friendlier."*

### 📣 Change announcements
Writes a clear announcement or release note: what's changing, why, who is
affected, and what they need to do.
- *"Write a note announcing the new expense policy."*
- *"Turn this list of updates into release notes."*

## Reports & documents

### 📊 Status updates
Turns your scattered updates into a tidy status report with a simple
red / amber / green health flag — for your team, a manager, or a client.
- *"Turn these updates into this week's status report."*
- *"Write a client-friendly progress update from my notes."*

### 🎨 Branded documents
Produces a polished Word or PDF in your company's standard look, with the correct
client logo already in place.
- *"Create this week's branded project update for Globex."*
- *Note: your team sets up the templates and logos once, so everyone's documents match.*

### 📈 Data reports
Turns a spreadsheet into a shareable report with the key numbers and charts — a
single file you can email.
- *"Make a report from this sales spreadsheet."*
- *"Turn this data into charts I can share."*
- *Note: a one-time setup by your team enables this.*

### 📄 Document summaries
Reads a long document, contract, or PDF and gives you the key points, the risks to
watch, and answers to your questions — in plain language.
- *"Summarize this contract in one page."*
- *"What are the risks in this document?"*
- *"Does this PDF mention cancellation fees?"*

## Your brand

### 📘 Brand guide
Creates or updates your brand style guide — colours, fonts, logo rules, and tone
of voice — in one clear document everyone can follow.
- *"Write a brand guide from our logo and these three colours."*
- *"Add a tone-of-voice section to our style guide."*
