# Proposal / SOW Outline

Client-centered structure. Fill top-down; the buyer's problem always leads.

```
1. Executive Summary
   - Their goal + our recommendation + headline value + the ask.
   (See writing-executive-summaries.)

2. Understanding of Your Needs
   - Restate the client's problem, context, and desired outcome in their words.

3. Proposed Solution / Approach
   - Approach mapped to each need. Feature → benefit → their outcome.
   - Phases / methodology.

4. Value & ROI
   - Quantified benefits (time, revenue, risk, cost avoided).
   - Why the investment is justified.

5. Deliverables & Timeline
   - Specific deliverables, milestones, dates. Acceptance criteria.

6. Pricing / Investment
   - Clear, unambiguous. Options/tiers if useful. What's included/excluded.

7. Why Us (Proof)
   - Relevant case studies, credentials, references, team.
   - Risk mitigation and how delivery is de-risked.

8. Terms & Assumptions
   - Assumptions, dependencies, out-of-scope, validity period.

[RFP addendum] Compliance Matrix
   | Requirement | Met? | Where answered (section/page) |
   |-------------|------|-------------------------------|
```

Before submitting: confirm every evaluation criterion is answered in the buyer's
language, and all format/page/mandatory-section rules are met.
