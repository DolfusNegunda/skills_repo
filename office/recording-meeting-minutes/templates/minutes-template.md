# Formal Minutes Template

```
[Organization / Committee Name]
MINUTES — [Meeting title]

Status:        DRAFT — for approval at next meeting
Date & time:   [date, start–end]
Location:      [room / platform]
Chair:         [name]
Minute-taker:  [name]

Present:       [names / roles]
Apologies:     [names]
Absent:        [names]
Quorum:        [confirmed / not met]
In attendance: [non-members / guests]

1. Welcome and apologies
   [Chair opened at HH:MM. Apologies noted as above.]

2. Minutes of the previous meeting
   [Approved as accurate / approved with amendments: ...]

3. Matters arising
   [Progress on prior actions.]

4. [Agenda item]
   Discussion:  [brief neutral summary of key points and any differing views]
   Decision:    [resolution, precisely worded]
   Vote:        [Proposed: X, Seconded: Y — For n / Against n / Abstain n — CARRIED]
   Action(s):   [see action register]

5. [Agenda item] ...

6. Any other business (AOB)
   [items]

7. Date of next meeting
   [date]

Meeting closed at HH:MM.

--- ACTION REGISTER ---
| # | Action | Owner | Due date | Status |
|---|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 |        |       |          | Open   |

Approved: ______________________  Date: __________
(Chair signature)
```

Rules: neutral, factual, past tense. Draft until formally approved.
