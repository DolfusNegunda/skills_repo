# Slide Outline Template

Fill this before building slides. One row per slide. The "Takeaway" becomes the
slide title.

```
# | Layout          | Takeaway (becomes the title)        | Support (visual/point)      | Notes
--|-----------------|-------------------------------------|-----------------------------|------
1 | Title           | <Deck title>                        | —                           | Presenter, date
2 | Content         | Agenda                              | 3–5 sections                | —
3 | Section divider | <Section 1 name>                    | —                           | —
4 | Content         | <the point, as a full sentence>     | <chart / bullets>           | speaker detail
5 | Two-column      | <comparison takeaway>               | A vs B                      | —
6 | Full-bleed      | <impact statement>                  | one image/chart             | —
7 | Closing         | The ask / next steps                | owner + date                | —
A1| Content         | Appendix: <backup>                  | detail table                | not presented
```

Rules:
- Every takeaway is a claim, not a topic.
- ~1 content slide per minute of talk time.
- Push detail to Notes or the Appendix (A-prefixed).
