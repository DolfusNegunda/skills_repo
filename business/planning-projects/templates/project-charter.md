# Project Charter Template

```
PROJECT CHARTER

Project:        [name]
Sponsor:        [name]        PM / Owner: [name]
Date:           [date]        Status: [Draft / Approved]

1. Purpose & objective
   [Why this project exists; the outcome it delivers.]

2. Success criteria (measurable)
   - [criterion + target]

3. Scope
   In scope:      [what's included]
   Out of scope:  [explicitly excluded — prevents creep]

4. Key deliverables & milestones
   | Milestone | Deliverable | Target date | Depends on |
   |-----------|-------------|-------------|------------|

5. RACI (one Accountable per row)
   | Deliverable / decision | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
   |------------------------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|

6. Resources & budget
   [People/skills, budget, tools.]

7. Assumptions & constraints
   [Timeline, budget, dependency assumptions.]

8. Top risks
   [See linked risk register — maintaining-risk-registers.]

9. Governance
   Reporting cadence: [weekly …]   Change control: [how scope changes are approved]
   Decision gates:    [go/no-go points]

Approved by: ______________________  Date: __________
```
