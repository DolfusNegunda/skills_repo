# Business Case Template

```
BUSINESS CASE: [title]
Sponsor: [name]   Author: [name]   Date: [date]   Decision needed by: [date]

1. Executive Summary
   Recommendation + headline economics + the ask, in a few sentences.
   (Lead with the answer — see writing-executive-summaries.)

2. Problem / Opportunity
   Evidenced statement of the problem and its cost of inaction.

3. Options Considered
   | Option | Description | Pros | Cons | Indicative cost |
   |--------|-------------|------|------|-----------------|
   | 0. Do nothing | baseline | | | |
   | 1. ... | | | | |
   Recommended: Option [n] — because [reasoning].

4. Costs & Benefits (economics)
   - Costs (lifecycle): [from cost-benefit analysis]
   - Benefits (quantified + intangible proxies)
   - NPV: __  ROI: __  Payback: __  (discount rate __%)
   - Sensitivity: [key assumption and range]

5. Risks
   [From risk register — top risks, scores, mitigations.]

6. Benefits Realization
   | Benefit | Owner | Measure | When realized |
   |---------|-------|---------|---------------|

7. Implementation Overview
   High-level timeline, milestones, resources. (See planning-projects.)

8. The Ask
   Specific decision requested: approve [option] and [budget] by [date].
```

Balanced and honest beats one-sided; a case that hides risk fails review.
