## Risk matrix (prioritize by rating)

```
        IMPACT ->    1     2     3     4     5
  L  5 |            5    10    15    20    25   <- critical
  I  4 |            4     8    12    16    20
  K  3 |            3     6     9    12    15
  E  2 |            2     4     6     8    10
  L  1 |            1     2     3     4     5
```
- 15–25 = Critical (avoid/reduce + escalate) · 8–12 = High (active mitigation)
- 4–6 = Medium (monitor/reduce) · 1–3 = Low (accept + monitor)

## Response strategies
- **Avoid** — remove the cause / change the plan.
- **Reduce** — lower likelihood and/or impact.
- **Transfer** — insurance, contract, outsource the risk.
- **Accept** — tolerate, with contingency + a trigger to act.
