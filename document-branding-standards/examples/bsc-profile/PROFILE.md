# Example Branding Profile: Business Science Corporation (BSC)

A complete, filled-in branding profile — included as a worked example of what a real organization's profile looks like once onboarded.

## How this profile was built
BSC had 3 real prior Lessons Learned documents (for Anglo American, DPM Metals, and Albemarle client engagements). The most polished of the three was chosen as the base template, its cover-page title/date were converted to `{{PROJECT_CODE}}` / `{{COVER_DATE}}`, its embedded client logo (`word/media/image1.jpeg`) was confirmed as the swappable element, and the identity table was tokenized for project name/code/manager/date.

## BSC brand identity
- Company: Business Science Corporation ("BSC")
- Heading blue: `#006395`
- Secondary navy: `#163E64`
- Accent teal/cyan: `#148AB7`, `#39C2D8`
- Accent red/orange: `#C20000`, `#EC5D34`
- Fonts: Arial throughout
- Footer: `© BUSINESS SCIENCE CORPORATION {year} | CONFIDENTIAL | PAGE {n}` plus legal/contact details

## Logo placement
The client/project logo is the one swapped per document (`word/media/image1.*`). The other brand images remain fixed BSC assets.
